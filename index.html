ABOUT ME

Hi there, I am Sanket Sinha, a self-taught artist, who works primarily in graphite and charcoal. My works include subjects such as mental health and its metaphors, social commentary, human emotions, and some portrait studies.

I believe the purpose of life and existence is to create things that essentially help people and helps to elevate the thinking of society. I aim to depict the surreal and abstract qualities of our lives and create works that achieve a deep emotional resonance.

Being inspired by surrealism and symbolism, I am working towards refining my technical skillset and using my inspirations to find a unique voice as an artist. I believe that art impacts the world and people immensely, so I want to use art to create a positive impact in the lives of people.
I pledge to create and consume good art for the rest of my days :)
